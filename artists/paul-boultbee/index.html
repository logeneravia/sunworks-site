Artist: Paul Boultbee

Art on Display in Our White Gallery

Paul Boultbee is a multidisciplinary artist whose practice spans painting, sculpture, drawing, and performance. His artistic philosophy is quoted as having a dual purpose: to explore his own creative spirit and to interpret the world for others. He studied Visual Art at Red Deer College, and is a graduate of Thompson Rivers University’s BFA program. Paul has exhibited across Canada, the United States, and South Korea.
